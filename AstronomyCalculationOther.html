Light Gathering Ability

Description:
Light Gathering Ability is the property of an optical system that tells you how much brighter things will appear than what the human eye can see. This calculation will determine how much more light one object gathers compared to another object.
Equation:
LGA = (d1/d2)2
  • LGA - Light Gathering Ability
  • d1 - Diameter of object one
