Light Gathering Ability
Description:

Light Gathering Ability is the property of an optical system that tells you how much brighter things will appear than what the human eye can see.

Equation:
LGA = (d1/d2)2
  • LGA - Light Gathering Ability
  • d1 - Diameter of object one
  • d2 - Diameter of object two
