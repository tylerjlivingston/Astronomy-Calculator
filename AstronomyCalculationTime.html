Description:

  • Currently, the fastest manned spacecraft is, N.A.S.A.'s Apollo 10 spacecraft, which reached the top speed of 39,897 KM/P (24,791 MPH)
  • The closest star system to Earth is Alpha Centauri, which is approximately 4.37 Light-years away.
