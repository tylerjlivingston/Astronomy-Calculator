Astronomy Calculator - Time


How long would it take to travel to another star system?

This function will help you determine how long (in years) it would take you to travel from Earth to another star system. For this calculation, you will need to know the average speed (MPH or KM/H) of the spacecraft that would be used and the distance (Lightyears) from Earth to desired star system.

  • Currently, the fastest manned spacecraft is, N.A.S.A.'s Apollo 10 spacecraft, which reached the top speed of 24,791 MPH (39,897 KM/P)
  • The closest star system to Earth is Alpha Centauri, which is approximately 4.37 Light-years away.
